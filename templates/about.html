Our Mission

At Plant Health Solutions, our mission is to empower gardeners and farmers with the tools and knowledge they need to keep their plants healthy. Our platform provides reliable plant disease identification, treatment suggestions, and fertilizer calculators to support sustainable agriculture and successful gardening.

Our History

Founded in 2010, Plant Health Solutions started as a small project to help local farmers diagnose plant diseases. Over the years, we've grown into a leading platform for plant health, serving thousands of gardeners and farmers worldwide.

Core Values

  • Integrity: We commit to providing accurate and honest information.
  • Innovation: We continuously improve our tools and resources to meet the needs of our users.
  • Sustainability: We promote practices that support environment & long-term plant health.

Achievements

We are proud to have been awarded the Green Innovation Award in 2022 for our contributions to sustainable agriculture.
