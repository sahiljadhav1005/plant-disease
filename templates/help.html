Frequently Asked Questions

How do I diagnose a plant disease?

To diagnose a plant disease, upload a clear image of the affected plant to our diagnosis tool. Our system will analyze the image and provide possible disease identifications along with treatment options.

How can I use the fertilizer calculator?

Enter the details of your plant and soil conditions into the fertilizer calculator. The tool will recommend the appropriate type and amount of fertilizer needed for optimal plant health.

What should I do if I need further assistance?

If you need more help, please contact our support team through the contact form on our website or email us at support@planthealthsolutions.com.
